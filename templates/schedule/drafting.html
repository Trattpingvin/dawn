What is drafting?

Martian Enterprise Tournament is a team tournament, and drafting is the process of forming a team. Volunteered captains will choose the players they want for their team. The drafting process will be streamed live, and captains can participate in the drafting process live or via pre-written priority lists.

I am a captain. How do I join drafting?

There are two ways to join drafting. First, you can join us live at the announced time. You can join us in voice call or in twitch chat. Alternatively, you can send us the list of players you want to pick in order and let us pick using the list. This way, you won't be able to react to other captain's choices, but you don't have to be available for the drafting day. You can find participating players' information at a glance in this spreadsheet.

How are captains elected?

Only volunteered players can be captains. If there are more than 4 captain signups, then they have to go through a voting process. However, if there are not enough signups, we may randomly choose from players who showed willingness to run as a captain but did not give us a firm intention.
