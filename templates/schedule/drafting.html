What is drafting?

This tournament is a team tournament, and drafting is the process of forming the teams. In a draft, each captain will take turns choosing one player that they want in their team until every player has a team. This process will be streamed live. As a captain, there are two ways you can participate in the drafting process. You can join us on the livestream via text/voice chat and do the draft live. This gives you the advantage of being able to react to the other captains' choices. The other option is to make a list of your priority of all the players. We will draft for you according to the list. We suggest using the information available in this spreadsheet to help with your draft.

How are captains chosen?

Only players who volunteered to be captains can become one. If there are more than four players who want to be captains, then they will go through a voting process where we open up a strawpoll and let the community decide. However, if there are not enough signups, we will do a random pick among the volunteers who did not give a firm intention of being captains.
